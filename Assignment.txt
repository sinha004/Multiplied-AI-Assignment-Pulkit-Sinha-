Problem Statement

What is a Near Miss?
In a construction company, a near miss refers to an unplanned event that could have resulted in an accident, injury, or damage, but did not, either by chance or timely intervention.

For example: 

A worker slips on a wet surface but regains balance without falling,

A tool falls from a height but misses people below,

A vehicle stops just in time to avoid a collision

1. Loads and processes the dataset (JSON to CSV conversion is allowed).
